Shuttled River Trips from the Mystic Lakes in Somerville

Experience the serene beauty of Mystic Lakes in Somerville with our convenient shuttled river trips. Enjoy scenic views, diverse wildlife, and peaceful waters. Perfect for nature lovers and families. Our expert guides ensure a safe and informative journey through this urban oasis.

Boston Skyline & City Lights Kayak Tours from Kendall Square

Paddle through Boston's heart at twilight, launching from Kendall Square. Marvel at the city's illuminated skyline reflected on the Charles River. Our guided kayak tour offers a unique perspective of iconic landmarks, blending urban excitement with serene waters. Suitable for all skill levels.

Moonlight Canoe Tour

Embark on a magical evening adventure under the stars. Our Moonlight Canoe Tour offers a tranquil escape on calm waters. Glide silently through moonlit landscapes, guided by experienced instructors. Perfect for couples, friends, or solo explorers seeking a unique nighttime experience in nature.

Boston Harbor Tour

Discover Boston's rich maritime history on our Harbor Tour. Cruise past iconic landmarks like the USS Constitution and Boston Light. Learn fascinating stories from expert guides while enjoying stunning skyline views. Perfect for history buffs, photographers, and visitors seeking an authentic Boston experience on the water.

About Us

Welcome to the Paddle Club!

At Paddle Club, we believe in the power of community and the joy of outdoor adventure. Our organization is dedicated to bringing people together through the love of paddling. Whether you’re a seasoned paddler or a complete beginner, our aim is to create a welcoming environment where everyone can connect, learn, and grow.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie among members of our community. We organize regular paddling events, workshops, and social gatherings that promote friendship, teamwork, and a shared passion for water sports. Our club is not just about paddling; it's about building lasting relationships and creating memorable experiences.

Join Us

We invite you to join us on our adventures! Explore beautiful waterways, participate in exciting events, and meet fellow paddling enthusiasts. Whether you’re looking to improve your skills, make new friends, or simply enjoy the beauty of nature, Paddle Club has something for everyone.

Together, let's paddle towards new horizons and strengthen our community bonds!
